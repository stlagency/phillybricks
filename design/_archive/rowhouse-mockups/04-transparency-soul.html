TEACH IN PLACE
Two terms worth knowing. An is a comp we trust; a is the one judgment call in our estimate.
ARM’S-LENGTH SALE
A sale between unrelated parties acting in their own interest, so the price reflects the open market. We exclude family transfers, sheriff sales, and $1 deeds from comps.
CONDITION ADJUSTMENT
A flat percentage we subtract when a home needs work the comps didn’t. Here −4% reflects a vacant property likely needing systems. It is the only non-public-record input, and we always show it.
