What this measures

The blends tax delinquency, open violations, and vacancy signals into one 0–100 score per neighborhood.

Distress index — a rule-based composite: each parcel's open public-record signals are weighted and averaged across the neighborhood, then scaled 0–100. It is descriptive, not predictive — every input is one click from its raw record.
