# Design context — Bandbox

(Source of truth for the `impeccable` design process. Product/eng specs: `PRD.md`, `CONCEPT_v2_shared_understanding.md`.)

## Design Context

### Users
An open-source, low-markup, community, **educational** market-intelligence tool for Philadelphia residential real estate. Two audiences sharing one product:
1. **Anonymous public / learners** — browse the market-scan map and property deep-dives; SEO-reachable; here to understand the city and learn how public-record real-estate signals work.
2. **Subscribers (small investors)** — use leads, alerts, and a mini-CRM to find off-market/distressed opportunities.
Context of use: daytime research; heavy reading + map exploration + data interrogation. Mission framing: empower investors to find value **and frame that value as community benefit** (rehabbing distressed/vacant parcels improves neighborhoods).

### Brand Personality
Three words: **Civic · Capable · Candid.** Authoritative and trustworthy like a great public-data institution — but warm, human, and alive, never bureaucratic. Empowering (makes you feel capable and fast at finding value) while community-minded (impact on the neighborhood, not just the spread). Transparent and educational: it shows its work.
Emotional goal: **capability + momentum, expressed through community value**, underwritten by **trust via transparency**.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Direction: Civic clarity** — institutional-but-modern, legible, data-forward; the data and the map are the stars; chrome recedes.
- **Theme: both light and dark, user-toggle**, each fully designed (not a dark afterthought).
- **The defining tension to nail:** civic credibility **without** coldness. Warm, crafted, human — the opposite of unloved government IT.
- **Anti-references (must NOT resemble):** Zillow/consumer-proptech slickness; generic admin-dashboard templates (identical cards + charts); crypto/dark-neon (glow on black, purple→blue gradients); sterile/bureaucratic gov IT.

### Design Principles
1. **Transparency is visible design.** Every number is decomposable and one click from its raw public record. Show the work; never a black box.
2. **Teach in place.** Inline explainers, plain-language framing, glossary tooltips — educate as you browse.
3. **Map and data are the heroes.** Chrome recedes; no decorative noise, no sparkline jewelry.
4. **Community-value framing.** Surface neighborhood impact alongside deal economics.
5. **Distinctive, not templated, and accessible.** Avoid every AI/proptech/admin/gov cliché; warm civic craft. WCAG AA, reduced-motion support, full light/dark parity.

## Chosen Design System — "The Survey Table, Warmed" (unified)
**Reconciled** from the "Rowhouse" exploration + the owner's brutalist `BRAND.md`, per the owner's decision to merge. Full spec → `design/DESIGN.md`; tokens → `../TOKENS.css`; mockups → `design/mockups/` (verified light + dark).
- **Visual language (from BRAND.md):** architectural brutalism — 3px ink borders, square corners (radius 0), offset HARD shadows (6/8/10px), visible grid; **Tanker / Zodiak / Satoshi / Space Mono**; concrete + patriotic-navy palette; third-gen South Philly voice.
- **Color discipline:** **true Phillies red `#E81828` = signal only** (distress, the one CTA, active parcel — 1–2/screen); **muddy brick `#A8341F` = text/edge accent only**; blue does the structural + data lifting. Light + warm-umber lamplit dark.
- **Mechanics (from Rowhouse, rebuilt in hard-bordered hardware):** per-figure source stamps → context rail; decomposable stacked distress bar; plain-English value-derivation drawer; teach-in-place glossary; multi-resolution 4-lens **blueprint** scan map; community-value framing.
- **Wordmark:** PHILLY/BRICKS stacked in Tanker; **PHILLY letter-spaced to equal BRICKS width** (measured after font load).
- All design/build work uses this system verbatim; `BRAND.md` remains the voice/logo reference.
